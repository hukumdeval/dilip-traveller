Our Story

A Yodha Transformed


What was once a workhorse is now a wanderer. Our Tata Yodha has been lovingly converted into a compact travel van — built for the roads of Bihar and beyond.

From the ancient ghats of Varanasi to the spiritual silence of Bodh Gaya, from the divine Ram Mandir of Ayodhya to the Jyotirlinga at Deoghar — Dilip Traveller takes you there in comfort, without compromise.

We're not just a cab service. We're your travel companion, your local guide, and your home on wheels.
